Caesar Salad

Description

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Chicken, sliced
  • Garlic croutons
  • Caesar dressing
  • Parmesan, shreeded
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Cut lettuce and chicken
  2. Put lettuce, chicken, and croutons in salad bowl
  3. Toss with caesar dressing
  4. Add shredded parmesan on top
  5. Add a squeeze of lemon
